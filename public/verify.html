Official Verification

Verify a Certificate

Enter the certificate number printed on the document to verify its authenticity, view the holder's details, and confirm its current status.

The certificate number is printed at the bottom left of the issued document.

How verification works

01

Locate the certificate number

Find the certificate number printed at the bottom left of the issued document (format: YYYY-NNN).

02

Enter the number above

Type or paste the full certificate number into the field and click Verify.

03

View holder's details and status

If valid, you will see the holder's full name, programme, date issued, and current certificate status.
